Jollof Rice

Description

There are many different ways to cook Jollof Rice. It is a dish made with rice, tomatoes, tomato paste, onions, red bell pepper, scotch bonnet peppers, salt, and spices. If it doesn’t have the above mentioned ingredients, then it’s not true Jollof. I make it the way my mum taught me, and I usually call it Nigerian Jollof because of the type of the rice that is used. Nigerians use the long grain rice that has been parboiled.

Ingredients

  • 5 roma tomatoes
  • 1 red bell peper
  • 1 onion
  • 1/4 groundnut
  • 3tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cups parboiled rice
  • 2 1/2 cups of chicken stock
  • 1/2 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 3 bay leaves
  • water as needed
